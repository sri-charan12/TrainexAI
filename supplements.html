Gym Supplements Guide

Whey Protein

Usage:

Mix 1 scoop (30g) with water or milk post-workout or between meals. Provides 20-25g of fast-digesting protein to support muscle recovery and growth.

Casein Protein

Usage:

Take 1 scoop (30g) before bedtime. Slow-digesting protein that provides amino acids over several hours to prevent muscle breakdown during sleep.

BCAAs (Branched-Chain Amino Acids)

Usage:

Take 5-10g during workout or between meals. Helps reduce muscle fatigue and promotes muscle protein synthesis during training.

L-Glutamine

Usage:

Take 5g post-workout and before bed. Supports immune function and helps with muscle recovery by reducing soreness.

Pre-Workout

Usage:

Take 1 scoop 20-30 minutes before training. Contains caffeine, beta-alanine, and other ingredients to boost energy, focus, and performance.

Creatine Monohydrate

Usage:

Take 5g daily (loading phase: 20g/day for 5-7 days). Increases phosphocreatine stores in muscles, enhancing strength and power output.

Multivitamin

Usage:

Take 1 tablet daily with a meal. Fills nutritional gaps in your diet and supports overall health and immune function.

Omega-3 Fish Oil

Usage:

Take 1-2g daily with meals. Reduces inflammation, supports joint health, and may improve body composition.

Beta-Alanine

Usage:

Take 2-5g daily (can be split into smaller doses). Buffers acid in muscles, delaying fatigue during high-intensity exercise.

Plant-Based Protein

Usage:

Mix 1-2 scoops (25-50g) with water or plant milk. Vegan protein source from peas, rice, or hemp for muscle recovery.

L-Carnitine

Usage:

Take 1-3g before workouts. May enhance fat metabolism and improve exercise performance.

Vitamin D3

Usage:

Take 2000-5000 IU daily. Supports bone health, immune function, and may enhance muscle strength.

ZMA (Zinc Magnesium Aspartate)

Usage:

Take before bedtime. Supports recovery, testosterone production, and sleep quality.

Citrulline Malate

Usage:

Take 6-8g 30-60 minutes before workout. Increases nitric oxide production, enhancing blood flow and muscle pumps.

Probiotics

Usage:

Take as directed (typically 1 capsule daily). Supports gut health and immune function, which can impact nutrient absorption.

Beef Protein Isolate

Usage:

Mix 1 scoop (30g) with water post-workout. Alternative protein source for those sensitive to dairy.

Taurine

Usage:

Take 1-3g pre-workout. May improve exercise performance and reduce muscle damage.

Electrolytes

Usage:

Mix with water during/after intense workouts. Replenishes sodium, potassium, and other minerals lost through sweat.

Ashwagandha

Usage:

Take 300-500mg daily. Adaptogen that may reduce stress and cortisol levels while supporting testosterone.

Caffeine Pills

Usage:

Take 100-200mg 30-60 minutes before workout. Enhances focus, energy, and fat burning.

Egg White Protein

Usage:

Mix 1 scoop (30g) with water or milk. High-quality protein alternative for those avoiding dairy.

HMB (β-Hydroxy β-Methylbutyrate)

Usage:

Take 3g daily (can be split). May help prevent muscle breakdown, especially during cutting phases.

Curcumin/Turmeric

Usage:

Take 500-1000mg daily. Powerful anti-inflammatory that may reduce muscle soreness and joint pain.

Collagen Peptides

Usage:

Take 10-20g daily. Supports joint, tendon, and ligament health, especially important for heavy lifters.

Betaine (TMG)

Usage:

Take 1.25-2.5g pre-workout. May increase power output and endurance.

Mass Gainer

Usage:

Take 1-2 servings daily between meals. High-calorie blend of carbs and protein for weight gain.

L-Arginine

Usage:

Take 3-6g pre-workout. Precursor to nitric oxide, may improve blood flow and pumps.

Tart Cherry Extract

Usage:

Take 480mg daily. May reduce muscle soreness and improve recovery.

Vitamin D3 + K2

Usage:

Take 2000-5000 IU D3 with 100-200mcg K2 daily. Supports bone health and calcium metabolism.

Glycerol

Usage:

Take 1g per 10kg body weight pre-workout. Hydrates muscles for enhanced pumps and performance.
